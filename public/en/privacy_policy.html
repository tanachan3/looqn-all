Privacy Policy

This app respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.
This policy explains how we collect, use, manage, and protect user data, as well as your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • User ID: We collect a unique ID generated at authentication via your Google account. Email addresses and phone numbers are not collected.
  • Location Information: Your current location is collected through your device's GPS when using the app. We do not obtain location data in the background or track you continuously.
  • Post Content: Text and other information you voluntarily submit through posts.
  • Usage History: Data such as posting dates and times, and which features you use. We do not track detailed activity like time spent or click history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and verify identity
  • To display posts and provide content based on your location
  • To store, display, and facilitate communication between users
  • To prevent and respond to misuse or inappropriate activity
  • To improve the app, develop new features, and enhance quality
  • To comply with laws and provide user support

3. Data Management and Retention

  • All collected information is managed securely to prevent leaks, loss, or unauthorized changes.
  • Unnecessary data is promptly deleted or anonymized.
  • Post content and usage history are generally deleted automatically after 24 hours.
  • If you request account deletion, your information will be deleted after verification and a short waiting period.

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not share your information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect someone's life, health, or property and obtaining your consent is difficult

5. Use of External Services

  • Google (Firebase Authentication, Firestore): Used for authentication and data storage. For details, see the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. If we start using them in the future, this policy will be updated to explain the purpose and your options.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, add to, or delete your personal information. You can also request that we stop using or sharing your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below. We may ask you to provide information to verify your identity.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated without notice to reflect changes in laws or improvements to the app. For significant changes, we will notify users through the app or other methods.
