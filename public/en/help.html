Help

LooQN is an app that lets you post and view real-time local information on a map.
This page explains how to use the app, the basic rules, and solutions for common issues.

About Your Account

  • Creating an account:
    Simply select your Google account after launching the app to register.
  • Deleting your account:
    You can delete your account using the "Delete Account" feature in the app.
    All your posts and comments will be erased after deletion.

How to Use

  • Posting:
    Enter your message in the text box at the bottom of the map screen to submit a new post.
    Note: The location of your post will appear randomly within approximately 50 meters of your actual location on the map for privacy protection.
  • Viewing posts: Tap the speech bubble icons on the map to read post details.
  • Commenting: You can comment on other users' posts using the reply box at the bottom of the post details screen.
  • Examples of what you can post:
    • Available seats at a nearby cafe
    • Information about local events
    • Current queues or crowd status at stores or stations
    • Weather, disaster, or local alerts, etc.
    * Posting personal information, anything that identifies individuals, or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.
  • Edit/Delete posts and comments:
    Posts and comments cannot be edited after submission.
    Comments can be deleted by the user who posted them.
    All posts and comments are automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • Photo/Image posts:
    Currently, posting photos or images is not supported.

Etiquette for Posts and Comments

  • Please be considerate of other users when posting or commenting.
  • Abusive, offensive, or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.
  • For details, please also refer to the Terms of Service.

Security & Privacy

  • All posts and location data are automatically deleted after 24 hours and are not stored long-term.
  • User information is managed securely.

Supported Devices

  • Recommended: iOS 15 or later / Android 8.0 or later
  • We recommend using the latest version of your OS and the app.
